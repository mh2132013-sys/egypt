atiste in music

am kalthom

Umm Kulthum (c. 1898 to 1975) was an iconic Egyptian singer, songwriter, and actress known as the "Voice of Egypt" and "Star of the East" (Kawkab al-Sharq). Renowned for her powerful contralto voice, she dominated Arab music for decades, with her monthly live radio concerts uniting millions across the Arab world

abd al halem hafth

Abdel Halim Hafez (1929–1977) was a legendary Egyptian singer and actor, known as "El Andaleeb El Asmar" (The Dark Nightingale). A titan of20th-century Arabic music, he revolutionized the romantic ballad with 80+ million records sold. His emotional style and iconic films shaped modern Middle Eastern pop culture, leaving a lasting legacy.
